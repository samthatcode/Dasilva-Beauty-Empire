Style advice & fashion coach

Can't find anything to wear in your closet? Do you no longer feel comfortable in your clothes? You have no idea how to dress correctly according to your body shape?

Did you answer yes to all of these questions? Then you should come to my style advice!

I do not offer classic color and style advice. Why? Because it only emphasizes your own type, but most of the time it doesn't improve it. There are often situations in which you have to change.
With your consultation, I would like to create a positive attitude towards life so that you can present yourself better and also feel that way.
There can be many reasons for such a change - a new love, a professional reorientation or simply a break from old patterns.
We'll take a trip to your wardrobe together. I will help you to select, sort or find new combinations that you have not thought of before.
The best way to experience fashion and reinvent yourself is to go shopping with me as your companion.

"You can buy fashion, you have to have style."

Together we will select boutiques based on your price expectations and your fashion taste.
I will take you into the world of fashion, where you will see and feel how you can better flatter your own body shape and how you can do justice to your desired style.
We want to bring out your body, character and style and that doesn't have to be defined by expensive brands.
We don't want to find a disguise for you that makes you stand out from the crowd. You should feel good and inspire others with your positive charisma.
